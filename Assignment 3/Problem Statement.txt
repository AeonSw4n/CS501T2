The loss in comprehensive understanding of security outsourcing will lead to more black box operations from government and therefore lead to a threat to democracy. For the above reason, there is a compelling need to discover the reason of outsourcing so that it will draw lessons for policy makers for future oversight and regulation. In order to classify whether outsourcing occurs due to legal, political, economic pressures on government agencies, Frederick S. Pardee School of Global Studies has been exploring on dataset of government contracts and FOIA request since Spring 2020. The research process in still on going.
