In order to classify whether outsourcing occurs due to legal, political, economic pressures on government agencies, Frederick S. Pardee School of Global Studies has been exploring on dataset of government contracts and FOIA request since Spring 2020. It is compelling to solve the problem so that it will draw lessons for policy makers for future oversight and regulation. Otherwise, loss in comprehensive understanding of security outsourcing will lead to more black box operations from government and therefore lead to a threat to democracy.
